Membership

Why be a member?

Membership gives you a trusted network and practical support that is difficult to build alone — especially when operating across two different regulatory, cultural, and business environments.

How to join

Three steps
Step 01

Get in touch

Contact us and we'll promptly share our Articles of Association and the membership application form.

Step 02

Submit your application

Once completed, your application is submitted to the Chamber's statutory bodies for review and a decision on acceptance or non-acceptance.

Step 03

Activate membership

On approval, pay the annual membership fee. Your company logo is then published on the website among the member companies.

Annual fee

One fee. Full membership.

Annual membership fee CZK 50,000 per year, payable on approval

After payment, your company logo will be published on the Chamber's website among the member companies — alongside the founding members and our official professional services partner.
